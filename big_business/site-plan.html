Overview

Purpose

My purpose for this website is to create a business website that shows off what the business is all about. I want it to show benefits of the company, useful information for people looking into it, and why people should choose this company

Audience

My audience will be other business owners. I want them to see a product or service we offer and how it can change their business

Dynamic elements

I would like pictures to automatically scroll on the page, as well as a login and/or credit card input
